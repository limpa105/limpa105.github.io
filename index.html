About

Hello! I am a first year Ph.D. student at Stanford currently rotating with Professor Monica Lam.

Previously I was at University of California, San Diego where I worked with Professor Nadia Polikarpova and Professor Taylor Berg-Kikpatrick on regular expression synthesis from only positive examples. I also worked with Professor Michael Coblenz on haskell debugging.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of Programming Languages(PL) , Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). By combining PL and AI techniques I seek to develop tools that boost their users' productivity, are sound, scalable, and intuitive to use.
