About

Hello! I am a fourth year undergraduate student at University of California, San Diego where I have the amazing opportunity to work with Nadia Polikarpova and Taylor Berg-Kikpatrick.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of Programming Languages (PL) , Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). By combining PL and AI techniques I seek to build program synthesis tools that are sound, scalable, and intuitive use.
