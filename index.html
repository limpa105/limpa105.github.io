About

Hello! I am a fourth year undergraduate student at University of California, San Diego where I have the amazing opportunity to work with Nadia Polikarpova and Taylor Berg-Kikpatrick.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of Programming Languages (PL) , Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). By leveraging the soundness of PL methods and the scalability of AI techniques, I seek to build synthesis tools that are easy to use, can generalize from a diverse array of specifications, and can be extended to numerous domains while maintaining robustness.
